Current Status of Child Education in India

Child education plays a crucial role in shaping the future of a nation. In India, however, there are still significant challenges that hinder access to quality education for all children.

Despite progress made in recent years, a considerable number of children in India are out of school or receive inadequate education. Factors such as poverty, gender inequality, and lack of infrastructure contribute to this issue.

According to recent statistics, the enrollment rate for primary education in India stands at 95%, with a gender disparity of 4% between boys and girls.

To address these challenges, initiatives focused on providing equal educational opportunities, improving infrastructure, and promoting inclusive policies have been implemented. However, there is still a long way to go to ensure every child in India receives quality education.

At ISHA, we are committed to making a difference. Through our programs, we aim to increase access to education, enhance the quality of teaching, and create a conducive learning environment for children across India.

By investing in the future of our children, we can build a stronger and more prosperous nation.
