ISHA - Indian Social Help Association

Welcome to ISHA!

ISHA is dedicated to raising awareness about the importance of educating women and children in India.

Our mission is to empower individuals and communities by providing access to quality education, fostering gender equality, and creating a brighter future for everyone.

We believe that education is a fundamental right that should be available to all, regardless of their gender or socio-economic background.

By promoting education for women and children, we aim to break the cycle of poverty, empower individuals to make informed decisions, and contribute to the overall development of society.

Through our initiatives, we strive to create an inclusive and equitable society where every child and woman has the opportunity to receive an education and fulfill their potential.

For a detailed analysis of the current condition of education for Indian women, please visit Education: Indian Women webpage with charts and diagrams.
